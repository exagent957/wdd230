HACC Membership Levels

Thank you for your interest in joing Hartselle Area Chamber of Commerce (HACC) where you can partner with other area businesses to make Hartselle, Alabama, the best home town ever.

Your yearly investment in HACC will grow your business. We offer several investment options to fit your needs:

Non-profit ($100)
Offers non-profits organizations a listing in our HACC directory and 2 free luncheon tickets to our monthly meeting.

Bronze ($200)
Offers your business a listing in our HACC directory, 2 free luncheon tickets to our monthly meeting, and free participation in quarterly HACC business training sessions.

Silver ($400)
Offers your business a listing in our HACC directory, 2 free luncheon tickets to our monthly meeting, free participation in quarterly HACC business training sessions and a free business spotlight on our website once annually.

Gold ($1000)
Offers your business a listing in our HACC directory, 4 free luncheon tickets to our monthly meeting, free participation in quarterly HACC business training sessions, a free business spotlight on our website twice annually, and a free listing on our digital billboard on Hwy 31 in Hartselle, Alabama.
