Typography - h2 example of Noto serif font family

(Example of PT sans-serif font family)
To keep things simple the site carries two fonts throughout. Both header and footer along with any h2 or h3 tags hold the Noto font family, a clean serif font, acquired from fonts.google.com. The remaining text on the site is displayed in the sans-serif PT font family also from fonts.google.com. The two fonts work well together. This page is styled with the font families mentioned above. The responsive design enlarges the font size progressively from small, medium, to large viewports.
