Site Description and Purpose

Client requested student developer build a responsive website of consistent design suitable for viewing on all classes of devices throughout for the southeastern part of Idaho to inform users of the current weather conditions, weather forecast, news and events in and around the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The site will include the following pages: Home, Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, Storm Center, and Gallery. Home page displays current weather discussion with corresponding weather image and information sections for the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Town pages will contain news, events, and other information and happenings in each town. Page footers will contain contact information, location map, copyright, author's name, terms of use link, and current date.


Site Title

Weather in Southeastern Idaho (WISEID). A shortened version of the title could be WISEID to match the domain name.


Domain Name

Developer proposes the domain name of wiseid.info which is available for $12 annually through Google Domains.
