Colors from the site icon are repeated throughout. The site icon holds brown earth tones, bright and light tones, and blues that should go well with images containing water or blue sky.

Page Background

An off-white page background and body, as seen on this page, provides a neutral slate to place elements. Bright white can be harsh on the eyes at times. The off-white is more subdued.

Site Header

Site header holds a high-contrast yellow background (#fdd900) with black text.

Primary Navigation

Primary navigation holds a bright blue background (#3e54b8) with white text. The hover property turns the bright blue into a brown (#9c5126) with white text. These two colors are very different from one another making navigation clear. Hover over the Primary Navigation bar to see the hover effect.

Footer

See footer below for example of footer color scheme. Light blue backgound (#26aaf7) with a bolder black copy for better readability.
