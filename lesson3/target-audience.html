Why visit Southeast Idaho

According to Visit Southeast Idaho, southeast Idaho frequently draws visitors to its natural beauty, hidden mountain lake, miles of hiking and biking trails, the famous Idaho Potato Museum, natural hot pools, fishing, boating, whitewater rafting, camping, snowmobiling, snowboarding and skiing. This site hopes is to draw the most visitors as possible, keeping them well informed and encouraging them to come to Southeast Idaho to boost the economy.

Primary Site Users

It is anticipated that the two main users of the site will be tourists and local residents. Tourists are likely younger generation adventure seekers, family groups on an outdoor retreat, or retirement-aged people enjoying the outdoors and local events. Tourists will use the site to plan activities and to stay informed about the weather and events. Adult local residents will use the site to stay informed about changing weather and local happenings.

Persona 1

Marc: A 64-year-old male married with an advanced degree retiree who accesses the internet daily from his home office desktop over a broadband connection for about 1 hour daily. He is tech savvy enough to navigate most websites. He likes to speak to a real person when questions arise. He enjoys the outdoors and seeks out information on things to do with his wife, festivals, and community happenings in Southeast Idaho.

Scenario 1

What is the weather forecast this weekend for Southeast Idaho? (Site displays a 5-day forecast)
What are current weather conditions in Soda Springs? (Site maintains current weather conditions)
How can I alert others about severe weather at my location? (Site has a form that user can submit to report weather events where they are)
What festivals are coming up this month in Southeast Idaho? (Site promotes current and upcoming local events)
How can I speak to someone about planning a trip to Fish Haven? (Site provides a telephone number in the contact us section)
Can I see a map of Southeast Idaho? (Site displays a map of Southeast Idaho)

Persona 2

Jacki: A 26-year-old female single self-driven college graduate and finance specialist who stays connected to her devices most of the time. She uses whichever device is most handy but primarily accesses the internet through her cell phone over Wi-Fi or a cellular network. She enjoys outdoor group adventures with her friends, looking for bargains, and small-town events.

Scenario 2

Are discounts available on group outdoor adventures in Southeast Idaho? (Site runs promotions at times listing group discounts)

Where are the deals on handmade crafts in Southeast Idaho? (Site promotes local businesses)
What are some good local restaurants in Soda Springs? (Site promotes local businesses specifically for the town of Soda Springs)
