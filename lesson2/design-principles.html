Design Principles

Marc Williamson

Contrast

Harbor Freight

harborfreight.com

The above website employed the design principle of contrast in the use of a red-white-blue color theme in the first ad and the use of and black-white-yellow-red color theme in the second ad. Both of these color combinations pop very well and draw the reader's eye to them. Also, note the contrast in the first ad between "5%, 5%, and 10%" which draws the user's eye to "10% in Harbor Freight Money".

Repetition

Tractor Supply

tractorsupply.com

The above website utilized the design principle of repetition nicely by making all of the shop or purchase clickable buttons the same style and color scheme (red rectangles with white letters in all caps). This allows the user to quickly locate where to click to see more items or to purchase a product.

Proximity

Costco

costco.com

The above website effectively displayed design principle of proximity by placing descriptive text directly below each item for sale. The user can clealy understand by this close grouping of picture to text exactly which picture the description belongs to.
